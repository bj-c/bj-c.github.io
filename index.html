Tell Me More

research achievements

I am a researcher in Image and Video Processing Lab., Seoul, Korea, and a Ph. D. of Internet & Multimedia engineering in Konkuk University. My research interests include digital image and video processing, computer vision, and video compression standards.

Image and Video Processing

Image and video error concealment, Image denoising, Single image super resolution, Filters for image enhancement

Computer Vision

Image and video inpainting, Image super pixel segmentation, Automatic object removal based on object tracking, Improve the accuracy of object tracking

Video Compression Standards

Fast rough mode decision for HEVC, Fast RDO skip for HEVC, Automatic control for real-time video communication, Monitoring system
