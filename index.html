research achievements

I am a post-doctoral researcher in Image and Video Processing Laboratory and I got my Ph. D. degree in Internet & Multimedia Engineering at Konkuk University. My research interests include digital image and video processing, computer vision, and video compression.

Image and Video Processing

Image and video error concealment, Image denoising, Single image super resolution, Filters for image enhancement

Computer Vision

Image and video inpainting, Image super pixel segmentation, Automatic object removal based on object tracking, Improve the accuracy of object tracking

Video Compression Standards

Fast rough mode decision for HEVC, Fast RDO skip for HEVC, Automatic control for real-time video communication, Monitoring system

temporal error concealment

We propose a hybrid TEC method to reconstruct frames with unknown regions that can be damaged in video communication. The proposed method uses the homography-based registration method to reconstruct unknown regions in video frames.

  • Project: Image and Video Inpainting for High Quality Video Communication Applications
    Sponsor: National Research Foundation of Korea (NRF)

error concealment combining inpainting and interpolation

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet consectetur.

We propose an efficient SEC method for the reconstruction of pixels that are lost in video communication. The proposed method is developed by combining exemplar-based image inpainting for patch reconstruction and spatial interpolation for pixel reconstruction using adaptive threshold by local complexity. By exemplar-based image inpainting, regions with regular structures are reconstructed. For complex regions with irregular structures, just one pixel is reconstructed using the proposed spatial interpolation method. The proposed spatial interpolation method performs reconstruction by selecting adaptively directional interpolation or neighbor interpolation based on gradient information. Simulation results show that the proposed hybrid method performs reconstruction with significantly improved subjective quality compared with the previous SEC and image inpainting methods. The proposed method also gives substantial improvements of PSNR values compared with the previous methods.

  • Project Title: Video Communication Technology over Next Generation Networks
    Sponsor: National Research Foundation of Korea (NRF)

Fast intra prediction method for RDO in HEVC

We propose a fast HEVC encoding method to reduce the number of candidate modes adaptively by adjusting the threshold for Hadamard cost (HC). The threshold is determined using the weight based on the standard deviation of gray level of pixels in prediction unit. The proposed method performs the RDO process using the reduced number of candidate modes having smaller HC than the threshold. Simulation results show that the proposed method gives about 16% complexity reduction with just 0.08% BD-rate increase compared with the original HEVC test model. The proposed method gives significant improvements of BD-PSNR and BD-rate with similar complexity reduction compared with the previous state-of-theart method.

  • Project Title: Live Encoder for 4K UHD and N Screen Real-time Broadcasting Based on HEVC Codec That Reduce Transmission Bandwidth Doubled Compared to H.264 Codec
    (KBS방송기술연구소, Thin Multimedia 공동연구 프로젝트)
